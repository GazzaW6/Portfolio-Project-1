about us

Nestled in the heart of Hoesdspruit, close to the Kruger National Park, Njovu Wildlife Photography operates an exclusive wildlife safari and photography package. Our field guides have multiple years of guiding experience and are sure to delight and entertain you whilst guiding you through the park. The owners, Luke and Zanelle have always been passionate about wildlife conservation as well as photography and enjoy sharing their passion for nature with others whilst also providing valuable photography tips and insight.

Njovu means Elephant in Tsonga and was born out of a true passion for Wildlife and sharing its beauty with everyone around the world. what to expect

We arrange day trips into the Kruger National Park including park entrance fees, pickup and dropoff from your hotel as well as guiding you through the park and providing insight and knowledge into the many facinating flora and fauna species that exist in the Kruger National Park. As the park itself is a wildlife reserve, we cannot guarentee sightings but we always endevour to give you the best possiblity to photgraph amazing animals including Elephant, Buffalo, Hippo as well as Lion and Leopards for those lucky enough. The trip also includes drinks throughout the day as well as a lunch tailored to your dietry needs.
